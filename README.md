# Statement of Ethics

Ethics provide the engine from which all of us make decisions, and thus are essential to living a life of prosparity. Running as a background process, they influence how we interact with others as well as ourselves. The core ethic that I live by is responsibility. I believe responsibility means being accountable for my actions, considering how my decisions affect others, and following through on my obligations with honesty and care. I value responsibility because any organization or profession depends on people doing their work with integrity and taking ownership of its impact. For that reason, actions such as ignoring risks, misleading others, or failing to follow through on important obligations are ethically unacceptable. In a work environment, behaviors based in avoiding responsibility, such as negligence, dishonesty, and carelessness, will damage trust and harm others, often undermining purpose of the work itself. To avoid these outcomes, I believe responsibility must be supported through clear expectations, honest communication, and accountability for decisions and results.
